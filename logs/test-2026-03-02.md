## Test 1: Health & Science Query

**Query:** "What are the health benefits and risks of intermittent fasting?"
**Result:** PASS

**Observations:**

  1. "health benefits of intermittent fasting research 2024 2025" — 8 sources
  2. "potential risks and side effects of intermittent fasting clinical studies" — 8 sources
  3. "intermittent fasting cardiovascular health long-term studies 2024 AHA" — 8 sources
  4. "intermittent fasting impact on muscle mass and metabolic rate" — 8 sources

**Issues found:**

## UX Fix Verification (Post-Implementation)

**Date:** 2026-03-02
**Fixes applied to:** `src/app/page.tsx`, `src/components/step-display.tsx`
**Tested with:** Same two queries from Tests 1 & 2 above

### Fix 1: Clear old results on new search

**Change:** Destructured `setMessages` from `useChat()`, call `setMessages([])` in `onSubmit` before `sendMessage()`. Added `searchKey` state to force-remount `StepDisplay` on new searches.

**Verification:**
- Submitted Test 1 query (intermittent fasting), let it complete fully
- Submitted Test 2 query (solid-state batteries) immediately after
- **PASS** — all previous results (search steps, report) cleared instantly on submit. No trace of Test 1 content remained.

### Fix 2: Show the query being researched

**Change:** Added `topic` state set from input text before clearing. Renders `"Researching: {topic}"` heading above results when not idle.

**Verification:**
- Test 1: heading displayed "Researching: What are the health benefits and risks of intermittent fasting?"
- Test 2: heading updated to "Researching: Latest breakthroughs in solid-state battery technology for electric vehicles"
- Heading hidden on initial idle state (before any search)
- **PASS** — topic clearly visible throughout research and after completion

### Fix 3: Collapsible search steps

**Change:** `StepDisplay` accepts `hasReport` prop. Auto-collapses into summary line when report starts rendering. Manual expand/collapse toggle via `manualToggle` state. Collapsed view shows "N searches completed — M sources found" with chevron.

**Verification:**
- Test 1: Steps expanded while searches were in progress (spinners visible)
- Test 1: Steps auto-collapsed to "4 searches completed — 32 sources found" once report appeared
- Clicked collapsed bar — steps expanded, showing all 4 search cards with sources
- "Collapse search steps" button appeared at top of expanded view
- Clicked "Collapse search steps" — returned to single-line summary
- Test 2: Steps auto-collapsed to "3 searches completed — 24 sources found"
- **PASS** — collapse/expand works correctly in both directions; auto-collapse triggers on report render
